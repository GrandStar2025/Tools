How to Create an Animated GIF

  1. Upload multiple images by dragging them into the upload area or clicking "Select Files".
  2. Arrange your frames by dragging them into the desired order.
  3. Adjust settings for each frame:
    • Set frame duration (how long each image displays)
    • Choose output dimensions
    • Select quality level
  4. Click "Create GIF" to generate your animation.
  5. Download your animated GIF.
Your images are processed entirely in your browser - they are never uploaded to any server.

Tips for Best Results

  • Use images of the same size for smoother animations
  • Adjust frame duration to control animation speed
  • Balance quality and file size based on your needs
  • Keep frame count reasonable for better performance
