How to Compress Images

  1. Drag and drop your image into the upload area, or click "Select File" to choose from your device.
  2. Adjust compression settings:
    • Quality: Lower values result in smaller file sizes
    • Max dimensions: Optionally resize large images
    • Output format: Choose your preferred format
    • Advanced options: Control metadata and optimization
  3. Click "Compress Image" to process your image.
  4. Compare the results and download your compressed image.
Tip: For web images, a quality setting of 60-80% often provides the best balance between file size and visual quality.
Privacy Note: Your images are processed entirely in your browser - they are never uploaded to any server.
