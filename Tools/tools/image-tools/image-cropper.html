How to Crop Images

  1. Drag and drop your image file into the upload area, or click "Select File" to choose from your device.
  2. Select an aspect ratio preset or use free-form cropping.
  3. Adjust the crop area by dragging the corners or edges.
  4. Use the rotation and flip controls if needed.
  5. Click "Crop Image" to process.
  6. Download your cropped image.
Your images are processed entirely in your browser - they are never uploaded to any server.

Tips for Best Results

  • Use aspect ratio presets for specific platforms (e.g., 1:1 for profile pictures)
  • Rotate or flip the image to get the perfect composition
  • Drag the crop area from the corners to maintain proportion
  • Use free-form cropping for custom dimensions
