How to Convert Images to Base64

  1. Drag and drop your image into the upload area, or click "Select File" to choose from your device.
  2. Your image will be previewed automatically.
  3. Choose your preferred output format:
    • Data URL - for direct use in src attributes
    • CSS - for use in background-image properties
    • HTML - for use in img tags
  4. Click "Convert to Base64" to generate the encoded string.
  5. Copy the output or download it as a text file.
Tip: Base64 encoding increases file size by approximately 33%. It's best used for small images (icons, logos, etc.) rather than large photos.
Privacy Note: Your images are processed entirely in your browser - they are never uploaded to any server.
