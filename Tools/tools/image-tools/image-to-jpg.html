How to Convert Images to JPG

  1. Drag and drop your image file into the upload area, or click "Select File" to choose from your device.
  2. Preview your image and adjust the quality settings:
    • Small Size: Best for web and email
    • Balanced: Good compromise between size and quality
    • High Quality: Best visual quality
  3. Click "Convert to JPG" to process.
  4. Download your converted image.
Your images are processed entirely in your browser - they are never uploaded to any server.

Tips for Best Results

  • Use higher quality settings for images with fine details
  • Choose balanced quality for general-purpose images
  • Use small size for web thumbnails and previews
  • Consider file size requirements for your specific use case
