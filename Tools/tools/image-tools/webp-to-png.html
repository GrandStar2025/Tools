How to Convert WebP to PNG

  1. Drag and drop your WebP image into the upload area, or click "Select File" to choose from your device.
  2. Your image will be previewed automatically.
  3. Adjust the compression level if needed (higher compression = smaller file size).
  4. Click "Convert to PNG" to process your image.
  5. Compare the original and converted images.
  6. Click "Download PNG" to save your converted image.
Tip: Use a lower compression level (0-3) for highest quality, or higher compression (7-9) for smaller file sizes.
Privacy Note: Your images are processed entirely in your browser - they are never uploaded to any server.
