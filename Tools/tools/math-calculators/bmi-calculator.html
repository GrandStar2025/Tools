Tips & Information

  • BMI is a simple measure of body fat based on height and weight
  • Choose your preferred unit system (metric or imperial)
  • Enter your height and weight accurately for best results
  • BMI is not suitable for athletes or pregnant women
  • Consult a healthcare provider for professional advice
